# Ego OS Company

## Company type

Personal digital AI company.

## Owner role

The user is CEO / Product Owner at the product level: sets goals, approves critical decisions, gives feedback, controls budget and evaluates results.

## Internal operating roles

- Orchestrator — understands task and assigns work.
- PM / COO — **planned role**, not yet runtime-active (`company/EMPLOYEE_REGISTRY.md`); today the Orchestrator itself maintains the task plan and status.
- CFO — tracks tokens, costs and resource efficiency; an ordinary capability-matched specialist, not staffed on every task (token/cost recording itself is automatic infrastructure, independent of whether CFO is staffed — `architecture/004_COST_AND_TOKEN_ACCOUNTING.md`).
- QA — checks quality before delivery.
- Employees — execute specialized work.

## Company rule

The company exists to reduce cognitive load, preserve context and turn AI work into a transparent, repeatable operating process.

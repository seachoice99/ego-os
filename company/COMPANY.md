# Ego OS Company

## Company type

Personal digital AI company.

## Owner role

The user is CEO / Product Owner at the product level: sets goals, approves critical decisions, gives feedback, controls budget and evaluates results.

## Internal operating roles

- Orchestrator — understands task and assigns work.
- PM / COO — manages execution.
- CFO — tracks tokens, costs and resource efficiency.
- QA — checks quality before delivery.
- Employees — execute specialized work.

## Company rule

The company exists to reduce cognitive load, preserve context and turn AI work into a transparent, repeatable operating process.

# ADR-0002: Employees are competence containers

## Status

Accepted

## Context

There was an idea to give employees career growth and personality-like development. This risks turning Ego OS into a game-like simulation rather than a professional tool.

## Decision

Employees are not personalities. They are versioned competence containers: role, instructions, tools, permissions, model requirements, reporting rules and UI avatar.

## Consequences

- Employees can be updated, not promoted.
- Models can change without changing product logic.
- Employee history remains stable across model upgrades.

# Finance System

## Purpose

Make AI work economically transparent.

## CFO responsibilities

- track usage;
- generate cost reports;
- warn about budget risks;
- recommend cheaper models/tools;
- compare employee efficiency;
- explain cost spikes.

## Main dashboards

### Task Finance

Cost of one task.

### Employee Finance

Cost and efficiency of each employee.

### Project Finance

Spend by project and workstream.

### Company Finance

Total spend and trends.

## Required fields for every execution

- task_id;
- employee_id;
- model/provider;
- input_tokens;
- output_tokens;
- other_usage;
- estimated_cost;
- actual_cost;
- timestamp.
